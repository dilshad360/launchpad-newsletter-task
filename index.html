Introduction

IEEE LaunchPad Kerala stands as a beacon of opportunity in the realm of technical and engineering pursuits within Kerala. A collaborative effort between IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn, LaunchPad Kerala is more than just a job fair. it's a catalyst for transformative connections. Its mission is clear to bridge the gap between talented individuals and innovative companies, fostering progress and innovation within Kerala's dynamic job market.

Call to action

Join us in shaping the future of Kerala's technical and engineering landscape. Whether you're a recruiter seeking top-tier talent or a student looking to kickstart your career journey, IEEE LaunchPad Kerala invites you to be a part of something extraordinary.

Event Roadmap

Participant and Company Registration

The journey begins with participant and company registration, where individuals and organizations alike sign up to be part of LaunchPad Kerala. This phase sets the stage for engagement and ensures that all stakeholders are primed for participation.

Challenge Start and Online Exam

Once registered, participants dive into the heart of LaunchPad Kerala with the challenge start and online exam. This phase tests their technical prowess and non-technical skills, providing a rigorous assessment of their capabilities.

Consolidated Rank List and Option Selection

Following the completion of assessments, LaunchPad Kerala consolidates a rank list of participants based on their performance. Participants then have the opportunity to select their preferred options, setting the stage for potential interviews and engagements with recruiters.

Confirmed Mail to Selected Participants

Selected participants receive confirmation emails, signaling their progression to the next stage of the event. This phase ensures clarity and transparency, guiding participants through the process with confidence.

Interviews

The culmination of LaunchPad Kerala sees selected participants engaging in interviews with recruiters. These interviews serve as the cornerstone of connection and opportunity, facilitating meaningful interactions between talent and organizations.
